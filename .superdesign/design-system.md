# Design System

## Typography
Headlines: Large serif (e.g., Playfair Display or similar elegant serif).
Body Text: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter), clean and legible.
Hierarchy: Strong hierarchy. Do not reduce font sizes. Large headlines, small supporting body text.
Line-height: Tight line-height, especially for headlines.

## Colors
Background: Warm off-white background throughout (clean, no heavy overlay).
Text: Strong black text for primary contrast.
Accent: Muted gold/beige accent (used in detail blocks or badges).
Pricing: Dark pricing card (center card in black or very dark gray).
Values: Do NOT simplify colors. Keep the rich tones.

## Layout & Spatial Rhythm
Grid: Mixed layouts vertically. Not a uniform grid. Avoid centering everything - use asymmetry where visible.
Spacing: Maintain dense, intentional spacing. Avoid overly tight elements, but avoid too much empty space.
Hero Section: Left-aligned typography with right-side vertical image. Overlapping beige detail block on the image.

## Images
Style: Architectural and workspace photography. Neutral tones, cinematic feel.
Avoid generic stock imagery.

## Tone guidelines
Modern, luxury, editorial, confident. NOT generic or minimal template.
